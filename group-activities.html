Group and Centered Based Activities

At Leadway Support, we are committed to enhancing the lives of individuals with disabilities and mental health challenges through our Group and Centered Based Activities service.

We understand the importance of social engagement and personal growth, and our carefully curated activities foster a supportive and inclusive environment.

We provide support with transition for individuals with complex, mental and disabilities, seeking help finding accommodation with accessible features to help them live safely and independently.

Our services include:

  • Diverse Activities: Our clients participate in a wide range of activities, including sports, arts and crafts, and more, tailored to their interests and abilities.
  • Social Connection: Engaging with peers encourages social connections, friendships, and a sense of belonging, which are essential for emotional well-being.
  • Skill Enhancement: Our activities focus on skill development, nurturing talents, and boosting self-confidence.
  • Fun and Enjoyment: We prioritize fun and enjoyment in a safe and supportive environment, ensuring everyone feels included and valued.
