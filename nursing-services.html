Nursing Services

At Leadway Support, we take pride in delivering top-notch Nursing Services tailored to individuals with disabilities and mental health challenges.

Our dedicated nursing team is equipped to provide a wide range of specialized care, ensuring our clients receive the highest level of support and attention.

We understand that each person has unique needs, and our goal is to enhance your quality of life through expert care and compassionate service.

Our services include:

  • Tracheostomy Care: We offer comprehensive tracheostomy care, including cleaning, maintenance, and troubleshooting, to ensure the well-being of clients who require respiratory support through a tracheostomy.
  • Gastrostomy (PEG/PEJ) and Nasogastric (NG) Management: Our team is experienced in managing gastrostomy and nasogastric tubes, ensuring proper nutrition and hydration for those with these medical requirements.
  • Continence Management: We specialize in continence care, including the management of Suprapubic Catheters (SPC's), to provide dignity and comfort to individuals with continence challenges.
  • Seizure Management: Our nurses are trained to manage seizures, providing immediate assistance and ensuring the safety of clients during these episodes.
  • Client-Specific Care and Equipment Training: We understand the importance of tailored care plans. Our nurses work closely with clients to develop individualized care strategies and provide training for the use of specialized equipment.
  • Oxygen Therapy: We offer oxygen therapy support, delivering the necessary monitoring to maintain optimal oxygen levels for clients with respiratory conditions.
  • Medication Administration: We excel in medication administration, including insulin management, to guarantee clients receive their prescribed medications accurately and on time.
