About Leadway Support

About Leadway Support

Welcome to Leadway Support, a disability support company founded by two individuals who are deeply passionate about making a positive difference in the lives of people living with disabilities.

Our journey began with our own personal experiences of caring for family members living with disabilities. These experiences taught us firsthand the challenges faced by individuals and their families, and fuelled our desire to provide support, empathy, and understanding to others in similar situations.

Our co-founder, a Registered Nurse with over 11 years of experience in acute, critical, community, and disability care, brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to our team. Their clinical background and compassionate approach ensure that our clients receive the highest standard of care tailored to their unique needs.

Our second co-founder has 5 years of experience and deep understanding of the day-to-day challenges faced by individuals with disabilities, complex mental health and forensics background. Their dedication and commitment to empowering our clients to lead fulfilling lives are evident in the personalized support they provide.

At Leadway Support we believe in the power of empathy, understanding, and support to make a positive impact on the lives of individuals living with disabilities and complex mental health We are committed to providing comprehensive, person-centred care that respects the dignity and independence of everyone we serve.

Whether you are seeking support for yourself or a loved one, our experienced and compassionate team is here to help. We work closely with our clients and their families to develop customized care plans that meet their unique needs and goals.

Thank you for considering Leadway Support as your trusted partner in disability support. We look forward to the opportunity to make a positive difference in your life or the life of your loved one.

Get to know about the NDIS

The NDIS, short for the National Disability Insurance Scheme, is an Australian government initiative designed to provide support and funding to people with disabilities.

Its goal is to empower individuals to live more independently and enhance their quality of life.

If you have a disability, the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) could help you get the support you need to improve your skills, quality of life, and independence.
