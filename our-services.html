Do you know the NDIS?

The NDIS,

short for the National Disability Insurance Scheme, is an Australian government initiative designed to provide support and funding to people with disabilities.

Its goal is

to empower individuals to live more independently and enhance their quality of life.

If you have a disability,

the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) could help you get the support you need to improve your skills, quality of life, and independence.
